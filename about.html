RULES AND CONDITIONS

No shows/Cancellations: Here at The Beauty Parlour, our clients come first. In order to provide the ultimate client experience, we require 48 hours notice to cancel/reschedule booked appointments. The Beauty Parlour reserves the right to charge a 1500 cancellation fee for no shows/late arrivals. Understandably, life can throw some unexpected curve balls– with respect to the stylist’s schedules and other clients looking to book in, please contact us immediately if you are unable to arrive to your appointment and we’ll happily reschedule it for you. If the 1500 deposit is not received at the time of booking or within 24 hours of the reservation, you will be notified that the appointment will be cancelled..

Products

KEVIN MURPHY

KEVIN MURPHY products contain high levels of natural ingredients and we use high grade essential oils and amino acids for the ultimate hair and scalp treatment. We consider the environment in all our business decisions and try as much as possible to find cleaner, more sustainable methods of doing business.

KOKO

A simple and complete patented hair colour system that offers sparkling shine and healthy hair. KOKO is an ammonia-free alternative that preserves the integrity of the hair. Superior shine and conditioning. Greatly reduces itchiness and irritations. KOKO is a healthier option for the scalp. Pleasant fragrance and better air quality resulting in a healthier salon environment for colourists and clients.

AVEDA

AVEDA is a cruelty-free brand. We do not test on animals and never ask others to do so on our behalf. Our products are “people-tested.” Being a cruelty-free brand is an important part of our mission to care for the world we live in and for those we live with, and has been since our founding in 1978.
