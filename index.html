Information

Sparkles beauty salon offer an extensive range of shampoos conditioners brushes combs scissors electrical items and accessories all with fast delivery. Pioneering inspirational and passionate for more than 50 years Sparkles beauty salon has been renowned as an innovator within the hair industry having grown from a single unit in clapham london to a multi national company.

Sparkles beauty salon is the official sponsor of the london fashion week and london fashion weekend. We offer a wide range of cutting and colouring techniques we cater for special occasions and have an exceptional range of hair and scalp treatments in our hairspa. With more than 475 salons worldwide a Sparkles beauty salon hairdressing salon is never far from you.
